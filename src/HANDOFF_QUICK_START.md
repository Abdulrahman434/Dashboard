# CareInn Handoff - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 In a Hurry? Here's Your 5-Minute Handoff

---

## ✅ 3 Simple Steps

### 1️⃣ Share the Prototype (2 minutes)
1. Click "Share" or "Preview" in Figma Make
2. Copy the link
3. Email it to your developer: "Here's the live prototype - interact with everything!"

### 2️⃣ Share the Docs (2 minutes)
1. Download all `.md` files from your Figma Make project
2. Upload to Google Drive or Dropbox
3. Share the folder link with your developer

### 3️⃣ Export Assets (30 minutes - can do later)
Follow `ASSETS_QUICK_CHECKLIST.md` to export:
- 4 logos (CareInn + Hospital)
- 2 badges (CBAHI + ISO)
- 17 app icons (WhatsApp, YouTube, etc.)

**That's it! Your developer can start immediately with steps 1 & 2.**
